# 🏡 KalmGhar — Rural Service Marketplace

KalmGhar is a mobile application that connects individuals with skilled workers such as electricians, plumbers, and domestic helpers. It enables quick, location-based access to services, especially in rural and semi-urban areas.

---

## 🚀 Overview

In many rural regions, finding reliable workers for daily or emergency tasks is difficult, while skilled laborers struggle to find consistent work. Existing platforms often fail to support short-term or location-specific needs.

KalmGhar bridges this gap by providing a **simple, accessible platform** where users can connect with service providers based on **location, availability, and skills**.

---

## ❗ Problem Statement

India's rural and agricultural sectors face challenges in connecting laborers with employers, leading to unemployment and underutilization of skills. Additionally, individuals often struggle to find immediate access to domestic help or emergency services like electricians and plumbers.

---

## 💡 Solution

KalmGhar enables:

* Quick booking of workers for **one-time or short-term tasks**
* Discovery of nearby service providers
* Better visibility and job opportunities for rural laborers

It promotes a **localized gig economy** tailored for rural settings.

---

## 🧠 How It Works

1. Users sign up as:

   * Employer
   * Service Provider

2. Employers:

   * Search for workers
   * View profiles
   * Book services

3. Service Providers:

   * List skills
   * Set availability
   * Accept job requests
